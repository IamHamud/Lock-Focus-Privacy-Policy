Lock & Focus

Privacy Policy • Effective March 6, 2026

Overview

Lock & Focus is a macOS productivity app that helps you pick a task, start a focus timer, and get things done. Your privacy matters — this app is designed to keep your data local and under your control.

Data Collection

Lock & Focus does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

All data stays on your Mac:

  • Focus session history is stored locally using SwiftData on your device.
  • App settings and preferences are stored in local UserDefaults.
  • No analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind.
  • No user accounts or sign-in required.
  • No ads.

Apple Reminders & Calendar

Lock & Focus integrates with Apple Reminders and Apple Calendar through EventKit. This access is used solely to:

  • Display your reminders so you can pick a task to focus on.
  • Mark reminders as complete when you finish a focus session.
  • Create new reminders from within the app.
  • Display today's calendar events on the Today page.
  • Optionally create "Focus" calendar blocks when a session starts.

This data is read and written only on your device through Apple's frameworks. It is never sent to any external server.

Notifications

Lock & Focus may send local notifications to alert you when a focus session completes, provide daily summaries, or remind you about your habit streak. These are local notifications — no push notification server is involved.

Widget

The Lock & Focus widget displays your active timer status. Data is shared between the app and widget using a local App Group container on your device. No data leaves your Mac.

Third-Party Services

Lock & Focus does not use any third-party services, SDKs, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Lock & Focus does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app does not collect information from anyone.
