Zero Data Collection

Lock & Focus does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No analytics. No tracking. No third-party services. No servers. Everything stays on your Mac.

Overview

Lock & Focus is a macOS productivity app for professionals and students. It integrates with Apple Reminders and Apple Calendar to help you focus on one task at a time through structured timer sessions.

This policy details exactly what data the app accesses, how it's handled, and your rights. It's straightforward because our approach is simple: your data never leaves your device.

What's Stored on Your Mac

Everything the app creates lives on your Mac and nowhere else:

  • Focus session history — task name, duration, timestamps. Stored via Apple SwiftData. Never uploaded.
  • Preferences — timer duration, notification toggles, integration settings. Stored in local UserDefaults.
  • Widget data — active timer state shared between app and widget through a local App Group container.

No accounts. No sign-in. No cloud sync. No registration. You own all of it.

  • NONE Personal data collection of any kind

  • NONE Ads or ad networks
  • NONE Cookies, pixels, fingerprinting, or tracking tech
  • NONE Selling, sharing, or renting user data
  • NONE Servers that receive data from your device

  • NONE Network requests — zero internet access, period

Apple Reminders Access

The app uses Apple's EventKit to read and write reminders locally. It can:

  • Show your incomplete reminders so you can pick a task to focus on.
  • Mark a reminder complete when you finish a session.
  • Create new reminders from within the app.

No reminder data is copied, cached, or sent anywhere. All access is local through Apple's frameworks.

Apple Calendar Access

The app uses EventKit to read and write calendar events locally. It can:

  • Show today's events on the dashboard alongside your tasks.
  • Optionally create a "Focus: [task]" calendar event when a session starts.

Calendar data stays on your Mac. The focus block feature is optional and can be turned off in Settings.

Notifications

All notifications are local (Apple's UserNotifications framework). No push servers involved.

  • Session completion alerts.
  • Optional daily summary at 6:00 PM.
  • Optional streak reminders at 8:00 PM.

All notification preferences can be toggled in Settings.

Third-Party Services

None. Lock & Focus is fully self-contained. No analytics, no crash reporters, no ad networks, no social SDKs, no cloud services. The only frameworks used are Apple's on-device APIs: EventKit, SwiftData, WidgetKit, and UserNotifications.

Network Activity

Lock & Focus makes zero network requests. No server endpoints, no API calls, no background network activity. The app does not access the internet.

Data Security

Your data is protected by macOS built-in security: FileVault encryption, App Sandbox, and your system credentials. The app only accesses resources you've explicitly granted permission to.

Data Retention & Deletion

  • Delete all session history via "Reset All Data" in Settings.
  • Uninstalling the app removes everything.
  • No external data exists to delete.

GDPR, CCPA & International

Compliant by design. We collect no personal data, so there is nothing to process, store, or transfer. No consent management needed. If you're in the EU, UK, California, or anywhere else — no data is held by the developer.

Audience & Children's Privacy

Lock & Focus is built for adults and professionals. It is not designed for or marketed to children under 13. The app collects no personal information from anyone, so there is no risk of children's data being collected.
